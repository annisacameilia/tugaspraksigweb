Interests

I have interest in Geographic Information Systems (GIS), Remote Sensing, and Photogrammetry. This interest is not just an interest, but an integral part of my vision and mission in my career.


Geographic Information System (GIS)

GIS provided me with an excellent tool for understanding the world around us by mapping and analyzing geographic data opening doors to opportunities in various sectors, from disaster monitoring, to sustainable urban planning.


Remote Sensing

Remote Sensing sparked my curiosity about how we can understand our planet through highly advanced technologies, such as satellites and drones. The use of satellite imagery for climate monitoring, land mapping, and natural disaster analysis gives me inspiration to continue exploring the potential applications of this technology.


Photogrammetry

Meanwhile, Photogrammetry is the ability to measure and model the physical world through images. This presents an interesting and fun challenge in creating accurate maps and 3D models that are indispensable in various projects.


My interest in these three fields has become the main driver in pursuing my personal career. I believe that by continuing to develop my knowledge and skills in GIS, Remote Sensing, and Photogrammetry, I can make a valuable and sustainable contribution to efforts to understand and safeguard our increasingly complex world.
